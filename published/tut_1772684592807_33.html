How to create an SMS campaign in Brevo

This tutorial will guide you through the process of setting up a new SMS campaign within Brevo. You'll learn how to navigate to the campaign creation area, select SMS as your campaign type, and give it a unique name. By the end, you'll be ready to configure your sending options and launch your campaign.
